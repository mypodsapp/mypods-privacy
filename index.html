MyPods Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 9, 2026

MyPods ("the App") is developed by Erkan. This policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Access

MyPods uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to scan for nearby Apple AirPods and compatible Beats devices. The App reads publicly broadcast BLE advertisement packets to determine device model and battery levels. This data is processed entirely on your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

MyPods does not collect, transmit, store on external servers, or share any personal data. Specifically:

• No account or login is required.
• No location data is collected or stored (Bluetooth permissions on older Android versions may list location access, but the App never reads your GPS or network location).
• No usage analytics or tracking identifiers are collected.
• No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.
• No advertising identifiers are collected.

On-Device Storage

The App stores your preferences (theme choice, notification settings) and the last-known battery reading locally on your device using Android DataStore. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

Bluetooth Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

• Nearby Devices / Bluetooth Scan (Android 12+): Required to scan for BLE advertisements from AirPods.
• Bluetooth Connect (Android 12+): Required to read paired device names to display your AirPods' custom name.
• Location (Android 11 and below): Required by the Android OS for BLE scanning on older versions. The App does not access your actual location.
• Notifications: Optional, used to show battery levels in a persistent notification and to alert you when AirPods are detected nearby.

Advertising

The free version of MyPods may display banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google's advertising SDK may collect device identifiers and usage data according to Google's own privacy policy. You can opt out of personalised ads in your Android device settings. A future Pro upgrade will remove all advertisements.

Children's Privacy

MyPods is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the effective date at the top of this page.
